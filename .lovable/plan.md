
# Demalt Group — Faqja e Re e Biznesit (New Website)

A modern, animated B2B website in Albanian for Demalt Group, a cleaning products manufacturer with 30+ years of experience.

## Branding
- **Primary**: Red (#DC2626) with dark charcoal (#1A1A1A) and white
- **Accents**: Warm grays, subtle red gradients
- **Typography**: Bold, modern headings with storytelling scroll animations
- **Language**: Albanian only

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Kryefaqja (Home)
- **Hero Section**: Full-screen with animated text reveal — "Tre dekada përkushtim, për produkte cilësore që zgjasin brezave" — with parallax background and a bold CTA button
- **Kategoritë e Produkteve**: Animated cards grid (Sfungjerë, Shtupa, Tel Enësh, Peshqirë, Doreza, etj.) with hover zoom effects
- **Numra që Flasin**: Animated counters (30+ vite, 100+ produkte, 5+ partnerë) that count up on scroll
- **Partnerët Tanë**: Logo carousel (SPAR, CONAD, Metropol, Elkos, Big Market) with infinite scroll
- **Dëshmitë e Klientëve**: Testimonial cards with fade-in animations
- **CTA Section**: Red gradient banner with contact button

### 2. Produktet (Products)
- Category filter tabs at top
- Product cards grid with hover animations showing product details
- Categories: Sfungjerë Enësh, Shtupa, Tel Enësh, Peshqirë Mikrofiber, Doreza, Qese, etj.
- Each card with image, name, and "Shiko më shumë" button

### 3. Rreth Nesh (About Us)
- **Hero**: Typography animation revealing company story
- **Historia Jonë**: Timeline from 1993 to present with scroll-triggered animations
- **Misioni & Vizioni**: Side-by-side cards with icon animations
- **Vlerat Tona**: Animated value pillars (Cilësia, Inovacioni, Besueshmëria)
- **Ekipi**: Team/factory photo section

### 4. Na Kontaktoni (Contact Us)
- Contact form (Emri, Email, Mesazhi) with animated field focus states
- Company info cards (Adresa, Telefoni, Email) with icons
- Embedded Google Map placeholder
- Social media links

## Global Elements
- **Navbar**: Sticky with blur backdrop, red accent on active link, smooth scroll on home page
- **Footer**: Company info, quick links, social icons, copyright
- **Animations**: Intersection Observer-based scroll reveals, staggered card entrances, text character-by-character reveals for headlines, smooth page transitions
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design with hamburger menu

## Animation Details
- Hero text: Letter-by-letter typing/reveal effect
- Sections: Fade-up on scroll into view
- Cards: Staggered entrance with scale + fade
- Numbers: Count-up animation when visible
- Navigation: Smooth underline hover effects
